Smart Recycling For You

The intention is for people to understand how to reduce, reuse, and recycle. Being able to buy new sustainable products for your life is not the goal; the intention is to use what you have now and understand the transparency behind what happens after the lifespan of your object is up!

CleanUp

Helping people make better choices one scan at a time. The little conscience you have at the back of your head and understand the effects of what happens when you purchase a specific product. The slight reminder of what you are actually purchasing and its effects on the world.

Let's work together to create an impact

Recycling is important, instead of producing more plastic, the compressed used plastic can be reformed again. Less than 11 percent of Canada's plastics get recycled. The rest end up in our landfills, lakes, parks, and oceans, destroying ecosystems and leaching toxic chemicals.
