Frequenty Asked Questions

Is This App Free?

CleanUp is free! We hope to create a global impact, one scan at at time. This means that we want everyone to have access to our app! While the app is free, it doesn't mean that we don't need support.
So, if you want to directly support us, try and collaborate with our team through crowd funding, or reach out for further details cleanup.sponsor@cleanup.com!

Can I Scan Barcodes and Products?

Short answer, yes!

Long answer, it's more complicated than that.
CleanUp is trying to train our AI technology to detect various types of materials. Currently we're working on collecting enough data for all products to be scanned just as the product. However, for a more accurate result, by scanning the barcode it will allow for more indepth details to be discussed.

Does it only scan plastic products?

No it does not. Products are made from various materials. It's important for our database to contain various forms of packaging materials to enable you to have all the information for your recycling needs.

Why is CleanUp worth using

CleanUp is a tool for everyone to understand the lifecycle of the products they consume. Finally allowing full transparency about global waste and the about the products you consume.

How can I support CleanUp?

Help CleanUp get into the hands of everyone by sharing our product and using our app. As a company we're starting to create crowdfunding initiatives and looking for affliates, so we can reach a larger audience. If you're interested in joining email cleanup.sponsor@cleanup.com
