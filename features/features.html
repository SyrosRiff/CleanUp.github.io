How Does it Work?

 Scanning  Products

Scanning products allows you to gain a quicker way to dispose of your products. First, once you scan the product, it provides you with three features. The feautures allow you to learn about the cautions of the product- such as warnings about various parts of the packaging. Also, allows you to learn about the type of plastic and its effects on the planet. Finaly, it tells your how to recycle, which packaging goes where, and discover the impact.

Tracking your Progress

The Tracking feature aims to track your progress, the items you scan are collected and show you your main categories of emissions. This feature also includes adding previous purchases, while using your personal data to track your carbon footprint. This feature aims to include data from the user input; however, it also aims to take the data from the user's spending history (either through a connected database ie: Paypal or ApplePay/ GooglePay or user input).

 More  Information

The purpose is for the user to learn about various different methods to recycle, reuse, and reduce. The concept allows the user to discover new information based on their data input and personal interests. This is for our users to discover their interests and learn about new environmentally friendly alternatives.

Help Solve the Global Waste Crisis.

We live in a consumerist society with products being able to appear within minutes of ordering them online. The app intends to be the little conscience you have at the back of your head and understand the effects of what happens when you purchase a specific product. The slight reminder of what you’re actually purchasing and its effects on the world.

Help tackle waste digitally for a cleaner future
